Account Deletion Guide

Data Deletion

When you request to delete your account, the following types of data will be permanently removed within 14 days:

  • User-Generated Content (UGC): All User-Generated Content (UGC) related to your activity in the application.

Data Retention

While your account data will be deleted, the following data will be retained for operational purposes:

  • Email: Retained to block further login attempts using the deleted account.
  • Advertising ID: Retained to ensure compliance with advertising policies and improve service relevance.
