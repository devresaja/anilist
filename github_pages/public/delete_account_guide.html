Delete Account Guide

Data Deletion

When you request to delete your account, the following types of data will be permanently removed:

  • Username: Your account username.
  • Email: The email address associated with your account.

Data Retention

While your account data will be deleted, the following data will be retained for operational purposes:

  • Advertising ID: Retained to ensure compliance with advertising policies and improve service relevance.

Guide
